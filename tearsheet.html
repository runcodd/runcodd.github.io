This page gives you an easy reference to some of the most common building blocks for Foundation. Modify them, play around with them, or totally destroy them to get going. Once you've gotten your fill here, check out Foundation's official documentation. There you'll find tons of example, instructions, and code snippets you can stick right into your prototypes.

Colors

Some of the color settings in the Foundation style sheet accompanied with this framework have been modified from Foundation's "out-of-the-box" configuration. The goal of this monochromatic wireframe style is to help your prototype not "look like" a Foundatin Site (or a Bootstrap Site, or anything else) so that you (and your users) can focus on the content and the relationships your structure creates.

Primary: Cool Grey 80%, #667788

Accent: Cool Grey 40%, #D1D6DB
